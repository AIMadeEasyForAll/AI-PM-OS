## How this was produced

1. Attached this repository to Claude.
2. Ran the prompt from [`commands/create-prd.md`](../../commands/create-prd.md) for a LinkedIn Saved Posts improvement.
3. Claude asked clarifying questions (evidence behind the idea, who it's for, what's actually broken) before scoping, per [`CLAUDE.md`](../../CLAUDE.md) and [`skills/feature-scoping.md`](../../skills/feature-scoping.md).
